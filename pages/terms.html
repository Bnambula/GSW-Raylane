2. Services

3. Booking & Payment

Seat Locking: Upon proceeding to payment, your selected seat(s) are locked in our system for 600 seconds (10 minutes). If payment is not confirmed within this window, the lock expires and seats are returned to available inventory.

Payment Methods: We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard via our payment processor DPO Group. All card transactions are PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant. Card data is processed by DPO Group and never stored by Raylane Express.

Payment Reference (MoMo/Airtel): For seat bookings, you must use your full name as entered at checkout as the MoMo reference. For parcel bookings, you must use the Parcel ID (PKL-XXXXXX) as the reference. Using incorrect references will delay confirmation of your booking.

Manual Confirmation: MoMo and Airtel payments are confirmed manually by a Raylane administrator within 15 minutes during business hours. Your booking status will show "Pending Confirmation" until verified. QR tickets are issued only after admin confirmation.

Prices: All prices are displayed in Ugandan Shillings (UGX) and are inclusive of all fees. International routes may also be displayed in USD. Prices are subject to change without notice.

4. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellation by passenger: Cancellations made more than 6 hours before the scheduled departure time will receive an 80% refund of the fare paid. Cancellations within 6 hours of departure are non-refundable.

Cancellation by operator: If an operator cancels a trip, all affected passengers receive a 100% refund to their original payment method. Raylane Express will notify affected passengers by SMS immediately.

Advance bookings: The advance booking deposit (20% of fare) is refundable under the above conditions. The remaining balance, if paid, is subject to the same 6-hour cancellation policy.

Refund processing: Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method (MoMo, Airtel, or card) within 24 hours of approval.

5. Passenger Conduct

Passengers are expected to:

  • Arrive at the boarding terminal at least 15 minutes before departure
  • Present their QR ticket or booking reference to the conductor
  • Comply with all reasonable instructions from drivers and conductors
  • Not carry illegal substances, weapons, or hazardous materials
  • Treat fellow passengers and staff with respect

Raylane Express and operators reserve the right to refuse carriage to any passenger who is intoxicated, abusive, or poses a risk to the safety of others. No refund will be issued in such cases.

6. Operator Terms

Bus operators who subscribe to the Raylane Express platform agree to:

  • Maintain a valid PSV operator licence at all times
  • Ensure all vehicles are insured and have a current road fitness certificate
  • Ensure all drivers hold a valid PSV driving licence
  • Submit only accurate trip information for approval
  • Pay the agreed commission percentage on all bookings made through the platform
  • Honour all confirmed bookings. Operators who cancel confirmed trips without valid cause may be suspended.

Commission rates: Starter/Standard plans 8%, Professional plan 6%, Enterprise plan 5% of gross booking value. Raylane Express deducts commission before releasing operator payouts every Friday.

Declared value: The declared value of a parcel is used to calculate insurance premium. Raylane Express accepts no liability for undeclared value or items excluded from the insurance policy.

Insurance: Parcel insurance is underwritten by UAP Old Mutual Uganda. Claims must be submitted within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery date.

Tracking: Tracking codes (PKL-XXXXXX) are sent by SMS to both sender and recipient upon booking confirmation. Tracking updates are provided at key stages of transit.
