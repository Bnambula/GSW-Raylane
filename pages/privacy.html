Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026. Raylane Express Ltd is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

From passengers: phone number, name, travel date, seat selection, payment method (we never store full card details — these are handled by DPO Group's PCI-DSS infrastructure). Optional: email address for loyalty account.

From operators: business name, URA TIN, PSV licence number, contact details, MTN MoMo merchant code, fleet and driver records.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to: process and confirm bookings; send QR tickets and SMS notifications; process payments and refunds; provide parcel tracking; calculate and release operator payouts; comply with URA tax reporting requirements; and improve our platform.

Data Sharing

We share passenger name and seat number with the relevant operator for boarding purposes only. We share payment data with DPO Group to process transactions. We do not sell your data to advertisers. We may share aggregated, anonymised route demand data with KCCA, NGOs, and research institutions.

Your Rights

Under Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019, you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your data. Contact: privacy@raylaneexpress.com.

Data Retention

Booking records are retained for 7 years for tax compliance. SMS logs are retained for 90 days. You may request earlier deletion of non-financial personal data.
