Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026 · Raylane Express Uganda

This Privacy Policy explains how Raylane Express ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our platform at raylaneexpress.com. We comply with Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019.

What Data We Collect

Data TypeWhat We CollectPurpose
IdentityFull name, National ID (for drivers)Booking verification, ticket issuance
ContactPhone number, email address (optional)SMS tickets, booking updates, support
PaymentMoMo phone number (encrypted), payment referencePayment processing and reconciliation
TravelRoutes booked, dates, seat numbersBooking management, loyalty points
Operator BusinessURA TIN (encrypted), PSV licence, bank/MoMo detailsOperator verification and payouts
Device/UsageBrowser type, IP address, pages visitedSecurity, platform improvement

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We never store credit or debit card numbers, CVVs, or card expiry dates. All card data flows through DPO Group's PCI-DSS Level 1 infrastructure.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making that significantly affects you.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and confirm your booking and issue your QR ticket via SMS
  • To send booking-related SMS notifications (departure alerts, delays, payment confirmation)
  • To process refunds to your original payment method
  • To calculate and track loyalty points on your account (if registered)
  • To verify operator credentials and process operator payouts
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations in Uganda
  • To improve the platform and prevent fraud

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number at booking, you consent to receiving transactional SMS messages related to your booking. We use Africa's Talking as our SMS provider with RAYLANE as the registered sender ID. We do not send promotional SMS without explicit consent. Standard SMS rates from your network operator may apply.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase PostgreSQL databases with Row Level Security enabled. All sensitive fields (MoMo numbers, URA TINs) are stored encrypted. We use industry-standard HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission. Access to passenger data is restricted to authorised Raylane staff only.

Data Retention

Booking records are retained for 7 years for financial and regulatory compliance. Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements). SMS logs are retained for 90 days.

Your Rights Under Uganda's Data Protection Act 2019

You have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data (where not subject to legal retention); object to processing; and lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Office of Uganda.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@raylaneexpress.com.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your search preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some platform features may not function correctly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • DPO Group — card payment processing (PCI-DSS Level 1)
  • MTN Uganda / Airtel Uganda — Mobile Money payment processing
  • Africa's Talking — SMS delivery
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Google Maps — terminal location services
  • UAP Old Mutual Uganda — parcel insurance underwriting

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies and security standards. We select partners who meet appropriate data protection standards.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via SMS. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent version.
