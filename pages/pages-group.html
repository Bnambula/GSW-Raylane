Group Booking FAQ
What is the minimum group size?
The minimum for a group booking is 10 passengers. For fewer than 10, please use our standard seat booking on the main booking page.
Can we pay in instalments or split the cost?
Yes. Your coordinator can set up a group payment link where individual members each pay their share via MoMo. Or one person can pay the full amount and be reimbursed by the group.
What if some people cancel last minute?
Individual members within a group can cancel up to 6 hours before departure for an 80% refund per standard policy. The rest of the group booking remains confirmed.
Do we all need to be at the same terminal?
All group passengers must board at the same terminal. We cannot split a group booking across multiple boarding points.
Can we charter an entire bus instead?
Yes — if your group fills 60%+ of a bus, a private charter may be more cost-effective. Use our Charter tab to get a full-bus quote.
