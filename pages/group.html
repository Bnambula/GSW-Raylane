Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum group size?⌄
The minimum group size for a group booking is 10 passengers. For groups under 10, you can book individually through the main booking page. For groups over 40, we recommend also considering a full charter.
Can we split the payment among group members?⌄
Yes! We can generate individual payment links for each member, or one link for the organiser to pay in full. Corporate groups can also receive a monthly invoice. Let us know your preference in the notes field.
What if some group members cancel last minute?⌄
Individual cancellations within a group are handled case by case. Generally, cancellations more than 24 hours before departure receive 80% refund per seat. Last-minute cancellations (within 6 hours) are non-refundable. The deposit is non-refundable once seats are confirmed.
Do all group members board from the same terminal?⌄
Yes, all group members must board from the same terminal. The one group QR code is scanned at a single boarding point. If your group needs enroute pickup, please mention this in the notes and we'll check if the operator can accommodate.
Can we book the entire bus (charter)?⌄
Yes! For groups needing an entire coach (67 seats), a private charter gives you full control over timing and route. Use the Charter tab on the Parcels page or contact us directly. Charter pricing is based on distance and vehicle type.
