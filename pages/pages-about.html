About Raylane Express

Uganda's First Vertically Integrated
Transport Technology Platform

Raylane Express digitises Uganda's UGX 2.4 trillion road transport sector — connecting passengers to operators, and operators to the tools they need to run professional businesses.

Our Mission

To make intercity travel in Uganda safe, predictable, and cashless — while giving transport operators the technology infrastructure to build sustainable, profitable businesses. We believe the journey should be as dignified as the destination.

What We Do

Layer 1 — Passenger Marketplace: Any Ugandan can book a confirmed bus seat in under 60 seconds, pay via MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, or Visa, and receive a QR ticket by SMS. No app. No account required. Works on 2G phones.

Layer 2 — Operator SaaS: Bus companies subscribe to a complete business dashboard: bookings, fleet management, driver compliance, payroll, GAAP P&L statements, and URA VAT reporting.

Layer 3 — Ecosystem Revenue: Insurance distribution, parcel delivery, corporate accounts, and data reports — additional revenue streams built on top of the core platform.
