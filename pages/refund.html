Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026 · Effective: 1 April 2026

We want every journey to be seamless. When things don't go to plan, our refund policy is designed to be fair, fast, and transparent.

Seat Booking Refunds

✅
Cancel 6+ Hours Before Departure
Cancel your booking more than 6 hours before the scheduled departure time and receive a full partial refund to your original payment method.
80% Refund
⏰
Cancel Within 6 Hours of Departure
Cancellations within 6 hours of the scheduled departure time are non-refundable. This applies to all regular bookings.
No Refund
🚌
Operator Cancels Your Trip
If the bus operator cancels a trip for any reason, all affected passengers receive a full refund automatically. We also notify you by SMS immediately.
100% Refund
⏱️
Trip Delayed More Than 2 Hours
If your trip departs more than 2 hours after the scheduled time and you choose not to travel, you may request a full refund.
100% Refund (on request)

Advance Booking Refunds

Advance bookings (made 7+ days before departure) require a 20% deposit to secure your seat.

  • Deposit: Non-refundable if you cancel within 72 hours of making the booking
  • Balance: If you cancel the full booking more than 6 hours before departure, the balance (minus deposit) is refunded at 80%
  • Full advance payment: Subject to the same 6-hour cancellation window as regular bookings

Parcel Delivery Refunds

📦
Parcel Not Collected (Before Bus Departs)
If your parcel was not collected before the bus departed, request a cancellation before 20:00 that day for a refund.
80% Refund
❌
Parcel Already in Transit
Once a parcel is loaded on the bus and in transit, no refund is available. Insurance claims apply for loss or damage.
No Refund
🛡️
Lost or Damaged Parcel
If your insured parcel is lost or damaged in transit, file a claim with UAP Old Mutual Uganda through Raylane within 48 hours of expected delivery.
Insurance Claim (up to declared value)

Refund Processing Times

  • MTN Mobile Money: Within 24 hours of refund approval
  • Airtel Money: Within 24 hours of refund approval
  • Visa / Mastercard: 3–7 business days depending on your bank

Refunds are returned to the same payment method used for the original booking. We cannot redirect refunds to a different account.

How to Request a Refund

You can request a refund in three ways:

  • Online: Log in to your account at raylaneexpress.com/account, go to My Trips, and click Cancel on the relevant booking
  • WhatsApp: Message us on +256 700 000 000 with your booking reference
  • Phone: Call +256 700 000 000 during support hours (Mon–Sat 05:00–22:00)

Request a Refund Online

Your refund eligibility will be confirmed by SMS. If eligible, refunds are processed within 24 hours.

Contact for Refund Queries

If your refund hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, contact us:

  • Email: refunds@raylaneexpress.com
  • WhatsApp: +256 700 000 000
  • Phone: +256 700 000 000 (Mon–Sat, 05:00–22:00)

Please have your booking reference, payment method, and the phone number used for payment ready when contacting us.
