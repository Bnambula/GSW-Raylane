How to Request a Refund

  1. Log in to your Account and click "Cancel" on your booking — automatic if within policy.
  2. Or reply CANCEL to your booking confirmation SMS with your booking reference.
  3. Or WhatsApp us at +256 700 000 000 with your Booking ID.
MoMo refunds are instant. Card refunds take 5–7 business days depending on your bank.
