Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Safe Travel

Every operator on Raylane Express is screened and monitored against strict safety standards. We monitor compliance in real time and block non-compliant operators from accepting new bookings.

Operator Verification Requirements

✓
Valid URA Tax Identification Number
Verified against URA database before account activation.
✓
Active PSV Operator Licence
Issued by UNRA. Expiry monitored — operators blocked if licence lapses.
✓
Vehicle Insurance (MTP minimum)
Motor Third-Party Insurance required per vehicle. 60-day pre-expiry alerts sent.
✓
Current Road Fitness Certificate
Per vehicle, issued by UNRA. Flagged 60 days before expiry.
✓
PSV-Licensed Drivers
All drivers must hold a valid Class D/PSV driving licence. NID verified. Licence expiry monitored.

Passenger Safety Features

Server-locked seat assignments: Your seat is reserved in the database the moment you book. No double-seating, no disputes at the bay.

QR boarding verification: Drivers scan your QR code before boarding. Invalid or duplicate tickets are flagged immediately.

Trip tracking: Passengers can track their coach live. ETA updated every 15 minutes. Delay alerts sent by SMS.

Incident reporting: Any safety incident can be reported to Raylane via WhatsApp. We escalate to the operator and, where applicable, to Uganda Police.
