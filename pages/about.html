Raylane Express is Uganda's first vertically integrated transport technology platform. We connect passengers to buses, and bus companies to technology — in one seamless ecosystem.

Our Story
Born in Kampala

Raylane Express was founded in Kampala, Uganda, with a simple frustration: booking a bus in Uganda required physically going to a bus park, standing in a queue, and hoping your seat was available. We built the technology to eliminate that queue — forever.

Today we operate a three-layer platform: a passenger marketplace where any Ugandan can book a seat in 60 seconds using any phone; a full SaaS dashboard that gives bus companies everything they need to run a modern transport business; and our own internal Raylane Express Fleet of coaches operating on the most popular routes.

Tusimbudde. In Luganda, this means "We travel together." It is our promise — to passengers, to operators, and to Uganda.

Passenger Safety
Your Safety Is Non-Negotiable

Every operator on the Raylane Express platform is verified, every vehicle is insured, and every booking is protected. Here's what we do to keep you safe.

✅
Operator Verification
Every operator must submit a valid PSV licence, URA TIN, vehicle insurance, and driver licences before any trip goes live.
🔒
Server-Locked Seats
Your seat is locked at the database level for 10 minutes during payment. No one can take your seat while you're paying.
📱
2G Compatible
MTN MoMo USSD push works on any mobile phone. No smartphone or internet connection required to book and pay.
⚡
Instant Refunds
Cancel more than 6 hours before departure and receive 80% refund to your original payment method within 24 hours.
🛡️
Parcel Insurance
All parcels are insurable via UAP Old Mutual Uganda. Declare your value and we protect it throughout transit.
💳
PCI-DSS Security
Card payments via DPO Group PCI-DSS Level 1. Your card data is never stored by Raylane Express.
👤
Driver Standards
All drivers must hold a valid PSV licence. Licences tracked and expiry SMS alerts sent 60 days before renewal.
🚨
Emergency Protocol
All Raylane operators are briefed on emergency procedures. Contact us immediately if you experience a safety issue.
Report a Safety Concern

If you experienced a safety issue on a Raylane Express trip, please report it immediately. We take all safety concerns seriously and investigate every report.
