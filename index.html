Help

Frequently Asked

How do I pay with MTN Mobile Money? ⌄
Select MTN Mobile Money at checkout, enter your MoMo number (0771, 0701, or 0750). You'll receive a USSD push notification on your phone. Enter your MoMo PIN and the payment is confirmed in about 8 seconds. Works on any phone — no internet required on your end.
Do I need to create an account to book? ⌄
No account is required. Enter your name and phone number at checkout. Your QR-coded ticket is sent directly to your phone via SMS. Creating an account unlocks loyalty points, booking history, and exclusive discounts.
How far in advance can I book? ⌄
You can book up to 90 days in advance. Advance bookings require a 20% deposit to secure your seat. The remaining 80% is paid at the terminal or you can pay the full amount online now.
What do I show at the terminal? ⌄
Show the QR code sent to your phone via SMS. The conductor scans it to verify your seat. No printing required. If you have no phone signal, you can also show your booking reference number (e.g. RLX-123456).
What is the cancellation and refund policy? ⌄
Cancel more than 6 hours before departure and receive 80% of your fare refunded to your original payment method (MoMo, Airtel, or card). Cancellations within 6 hours are non-refundable. Refunds are processed within 24 hours.
How do I track my parcel? ⌄
After booking, both sender and recipient receive an SMS with a tracking code (e.g. PKL-123456). Visit raylaneexpress.com/parcels, click "Track Parcel", enter your code and see live status: Picked Up → Verified → In Transit → Delivered.
How quickly can my bus company go live? ⌄
Submit your application at raylaneexpress.com/partner. Our onboarding team reviews documents within 24 hours. Once approved, you receive your dashboard credentials via SMS and can submit your first trip immediately. Most operators go live within 48 hours.
