Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking, payments, cancellations, parcels, and more.

Can't find your answer?

WhatsApp Support
Do I need to create an account to book?
No account is required. You only need your mobile number. After payment, your QR ticket is sent via SMS. An account is optional — create one to earn loyalty points and view booking history.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept MTN Mobile Money (USSD push — works on any phone including 2G feature phones), Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard. All card payments go through DPO Group's PCI-DSS Level 1 payment gateway with 3D Secure.
How does MTN MoMo payment work?
After confirming your booking, enter your MTN number (0771, 0701, or 0750). You'll receive a USSD prompt on your phone asking you to approve the payment with your MoMo PIN. Confirmation arrives in approximately 8 seconds. No internet or smartphone needed.
How long is my seat held before payment?
Your selected seat is locked in our database for 10 minutes from the moment you proceed to payment. If payment is not received within 10 minutes, the seat is automatically released back to available inventory.
Can I book for someone else?
Yes. Enter the traveller's name and phone number during booking. The QR ticket will be sent to the phone number provided. Any valid MTN, Airtel, or card can be used for payment regardless of whose name is on the ticket.
What is advance booking?
Advance booking lets you reserve a seat up to 30 days ahead by paying a 20% commitment deposit. Your price is locked at booking date. The remaining 80% is collected via SMS payment link 24 hours before departure. Cancel 48+ hours before for a full deposit refund.
Can I be picked up along the route instead of at the terminal?
Yes. Select "Pick Me Up Enroute" in the boarding preference when searching. Enter your landmark or location on the route. The operator will confirm if there is a designated enroute stop near you — not all stops are guaranteed. The driver will contact you 30 minutes before passing your location.
Can I be dropped off before the final destination?
At this time, drop-off at intermediate points is not available through the online booking system. Please contact us on WhatsApp to arrange special alighting points for your journey — these are handled case by case with the operator.
How do I board the bus at the terminal?
Show the QR code from your SMS ticket to the driver or conductor. They scan it with the Raylane driver app — green screen confirms valid, red screen indicates an issue. You'll be assigned your exact seat number as shown on your ticket.
What if my coach is delayed?
You will receive an SMS notification if your coach is delayed by more than 30 minutes. The updated ETA will be in the message. You can also track your coach live on our website using your booking reference.
Is luggage included in my ticket?
One medium bag in the luggage hold and one small bag in the cabin are included. Excess luggage or oversized items may be charged separately by the operator at the terminal. Heavy cargo must be pre-booked as a Parcel.
What is the cancellation and refund policy?
Standard bookings: Cancel 6+ hours before departure for an 80% refund to your original payment method. Cancellations within 6 hours of departure are non-refundable. Advance bookings: Cancel 48+ hours before departure for a full commitment fee refund. Refunds are processed within 15 minutes for MoMo and within 5-7 business days for card payments.
What happens if the operator cancels the trip?
If an operator cancels a trip, you receive a 100% refund automatically to your original payment method, plus an SMS notification with full details. You also receive a voucher code for UGX 5,000 off your next booking as a goodwill gesture.
How do I cancel my booking?
Log in to My Account and click "Cancel" on your booking. Alternatively, reply CANCEL to your booking confirmation SMS, or contact us on WhatsApp with your booking reference. Refunds are processed immediately upon cancellation if within policy.
Can I change the date or seat after booking?
Date changes are available up to 24 hours before departure for a UGX 3,000 change fee, subject to seat availability. Seat changes on the same trip are free if done more than 2 hours before departure. Contact our WhatsApp support for all changes.
How do I send a parcel with Raylane?
Book online or visit any Raylane terminal. Select "Send Parcel" tab, choose the parcel category, enter sender and recipient details, pay via MoMo or card. Your parcel receives a unique tracking code (PKL-xxxxxx). Drop off at the terminal before the bus departs, or add the Pickup Rider option for UGX 5,000 and we collect from your location.
How do I track my parcel?
Enter your PKL tracking code in the Track Your Parcel section on this page, or reply TRACK to any SMS from us. Both the sender and recipient receive live SMS updates at each status change. The ETA is updated every 15 minutes while in transit.
Is parcel insurance available?
Yes. Add Parcel Insurance during booking at 3% of the declared item value. Coverage includes loss, theft, and damage in transit. Underwritten by UAP Old Mutual Uganda. Claims require: tracking number, item description, police report for theft, and photos. Average settlement is 5 business days.
What items cannot be sent as parcels?
Prohibited items include: cash or bearer instruments, firearms and ammunition, flammable or explosive materials, illegal substances, live animals, perishable food without special arrangement, and items over 50kg (requires special cargo booking). Raylane reserves the right to inspect parcels at any point during transit.
How do I apply to become a Raylane operator?
Visit raylaneexpress.com/partner and complete the three-step application form. You'll need: company name, contact details, URA TIN, PSV operator licence, fleet size, vehicle registration details, and driver licence copies. Applications are reviewed within 48 hours.
Can I customise the operator dashboard with my company branding?
Yes. Professional and Enterprise plan operators can upload their company logo and set a primary brand colour in Settings → Branding. Your custom logo and colours appear throughout your operator dashboard and on passenger-facing booking confirmation pages for trips on your buses.
How and when do I get paid?
Payouts are processed weekly every Friday. Admin reviews and releases payment to your MTN MoMo merchant code via B2B transfer. You receive an SMS receipt from Raylane detailing the route, gross amount, commission deducted, and net paid. You can also see the full payout breakdown in your Payments tab before release.
What is the commission rate?
Starter and Standard plans: 8% on all confirmed bookings. Professional plan: 6%. Enterprise plan: 5%. Commission is calculated on the gross booking value before any promotions. Parcel revenue shares the same commission rate as seat bookings.
How do loyalty points work?
Earn 1 point per UGX 100 spent on Standard bookings. Silver members earn 1.5× and Gold members earn 2×. Points accumulate over time and unlock tier benefits automatically: Silver at 2,000 points, Gold at 10,000 points. Redeem points for discounts on future bookings from your Account page.
How do I create an account?
Visit My Account and enter your mobile number. You'll receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS to verify. No email or password required. Your booking history is linked to your phone number automatically.
Is my personal data shared with operators?
Operators receive only the information required to process your journey: name, seat number, departure point, and destination. Payment details are never shared. Your phone number is visible to the operator for journey-related communications only. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
